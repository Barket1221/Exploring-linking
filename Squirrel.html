A SQUIRREL?

With 5 seconds left on the clock you turn and hurl the ball seemingly toward nothing, when just before the ball hits the ground it starts floating toward the endzone. The ball flies down the field and into the endzone then to everyone's surprise a small squirel in a red jersey comes out from under the ball. The refs and crowd are stunned, but because the ball never touched the ground they have no choice but to call it a touchdown. Your team rejoices as you hoist this tiny squirell into the air! tonight we celebrate as champs!

YOU WIN!
